Face deformation


Facedeform preset

This is a demo of real-time face deformation with a parametric model using the javascript library clmtrackr. Click start, keep your face still until the facemodel has fitted and try selecting some presets or adjusting the parameters on the right hand side.

Note that this demo works best with good, even lighting. The demo also needs support for WebGL, and works best in Google Chrome.
